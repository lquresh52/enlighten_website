About Us

Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) have revolutionized the light industry over the past decade. Not only has it ushered in an era of unprecedented efficiency, longevity and safety while also being great for the environment, it has also challenged long established notions of lighting design.

LED Lights have permitted designers to apply lighting in clever and creative ways which was never an option for humankind in the past. From customized profile and linear lights to circular lights, from ambient lighting to focused spot lighting, from direct glaring flood lighting to soft indirect cove lighting and from invisible rimless recessed lights to beautiful hanging pendants, LEDs have enabled new ways of imagining lighting.

In this ever changing and exciting lighting industry, Enlighten ProTM strives to be the go-to choice of designers everywhere. We hope to achieve that by offering innovative lights which balance high quality and cost effectiveness to bring value to our end users. We use grade-A LEDs and drivers in our products, which are procured from leading brands across the world.

In support of the ‘Make in India’ campaign, and to move India to a net exporting country within this decade, Enlighten ProTM has made a decision to manufacture all lights entirely in-house. The manufacturing capabilities that we have developed allow us to provide incredible quality control, unmatched customization and end-to-end support to our customers.

Every day, we are trying to incorporate more systems, more automation, more capabilities and more quality control to our organization which will enable us to serve our customers even better.
